Return to your homeland with 300 golden coins, collected through the crumbling corridors of an ancient kingdom. With each corridor being more dangerous than the previous, you shouldn't waste your time.

Equipment

Hellfire Shield

Though resembling a tool of protection, it is not. This ancient artifact bears a portal to hell on its front surface, through which you can summon the burning souls of the fallen knights before you. Who knows, you might be one of them soon.

Straight Sword

A standard straight sword.

Controls

Keyboard controls

S to use sword
D to use shield
SPACEBAR to jump
LEFT & RIGHT ARROWS to move

Touch controls

Tap the start screen on your touch device to reveal the touch controls. Depending on your browser and device, the touch controls either work great or piss you off, apologies for that.

Resetting the game

After completing each corridor, your progress is saved and you can continue from where you left off. Should you feel the need to start from the beginning, do the following while in gameplay (your progress will be lost!):

Keyboard: press and hold down the R button for two seconds.
Touch screen: touch and hold the game screen for two seconds.
